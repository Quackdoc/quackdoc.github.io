The TV Friendly Linux Databse

People spend most of their time infront of screens. This is no surprise, Open source sofrware is making strides on being part of everyones life. Desktops and smart devices make up over 50% of screen time for the average adult. However linux hasn't made way in terms of television, where people can spend anywhere from 20% to 60% of their time.

The goal of The TV Friendly Linux Databse is to list applications that are not only compatible but also a good experience to use on the device where we spend most of our time.

The Shell

One should not underestimate the importance of the shell. Think of the shell as a map. If the Map is no good, one can not navigate well. as such the importance of the shell to not only be flawless in execution, but also in appearance and feel is high.

Applications

Applications are the lifeblood of an operating system. Without them, no end user would use the system it is important for the needs of such a system to be filled by applications that work without "Jank" This means that the applications need to be controlled, to the extent the average user would ever do, fully by means of a device, that fits the want's of a user.

This can best be accomplished by way of remote controll, or a console's controller.
